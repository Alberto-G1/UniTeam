Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about UniTeam. Can't find your answer? Reach out to us.

What is UniTeam and who is it for?
UniTeam is a collaborative project management platform built specifically for university environments. It's designed for students working on group assignments and dissertations, lecturers supervising multiple projects, and admins managing departmental submissions.
Is UniTeam free to use?
Yes! UniTeam offers a free Starter plan that includes up to 3 projects, 5 team members, and 500MB storage — no credit card required. Paid Pro and Institution plans unlock unlimited projects, advanced analytics, and more.
Can my lecturer or supervisor access my projects?
Absolutely. You can invite your lecturer or supervisor to your project with a Supervisor role, giving them read-only or comment access to monitor progress, leave feedback, and approve submissions without being able to edit your work.
Does UniTeam integrate with Moodle, Blackboard, or Turnitin?
Yes — our Institution plan includes LMS integrations with Moodle, Blackboard, and Canvas. Turnitin integration is available on all paid plans, allowing you to submit directly for plagiarism checks from within UniTeam.
How secure is my data on UniTeam?
All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We are GDPR compliant and never sell or share student data with third parties. Institutional accounts include additional data residency options.
Can I use UniTeam on my phone?
Yes. UniTeam has native iOS and Android apps available for download. The web app is also fully responsive and works great on mobile browsers. We've optimised for low-bandwidth environments common in many African university settings.
How do I get my university set up on UniTeam?
Contact our institutional sales team via the Contact page. We'll set up a demo, create a pilot programme for one department, and provide full onboarding support for staff and students at no cost during the trial period.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to help. Reach out anytime.
